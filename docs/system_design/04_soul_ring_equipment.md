# Soul Ring & Equipment Systems

## Soul Ring System (Core Progression)

### Hunt Mechanic Flow
```
Enter Hunting Zone → Spawn Beast → Kill → Absorb → RNG Roll
```

**RNG Layers**:
1. Beast Type (Random from zone pool)
2. Age/Years (Random within tier bounds)
3. Skill Granted (Random from beast's skill pool)

### Age Tiers & Multipliers

| Tier | Years | Color | Source | Stat Mult | Skill Quality |
|------|-------|-------|--------|-----------|---------------|
| White | 10-100 | White | Common Mobs | 1.0x | Basic Passive |
| Yellow | 100-999 | Yellow | Elite Mobs | 1.2x | Active Skill I |
| Purple | 1,000-9,999 | Purple | Dungeon Boss | 1.5x | Active Skill II |
| Black | 10k-99,999 | Black | World Boss | 2.5x | Active III + Stat |
| Red | 100,000+ | Red | Event/Gacha | 5.0x | God Skill + Unique |
| Gold | 1M+ | Gold | Whale Exclusive | 10.0x | Reality Bending |

### Perfect Ring Chase Psychology
**Ideal Ring**: 100,000 Year + Specific Skill + Crit DMG Sub + ATK% Sub

**Failure States Drive Spending**:
- Wrong year level → Use Fusion items (Gold sink)
- Wrong skill → Use Rewrite scrolls (Gacha sink)  
- Wrong substats → Reroll materials (Resource sink)

**Near Miss Effect**: Getting Black ring with wrong skill hurts more than Purple ring - drives "one more try" compulsion.

## Equipment System

### Slot Configuration
Weapon, Armor, Helm, Pants, Boots, Gloves, Necklace, 2 Rings, Artifact

### Set Bonus Structure
- (2) Piece: +10% ATK
- (4) Piece: +20% Crit  
- (6) Piece: Ultimate CD -30%

**Design Goal**: Force collection of multiple pieces, prevent one-and-done satisfaction.

## Upgrade Layers (Infinite Grind)

### 1. Enhancement (+1 to +15)
- Cost: Gold
- Success: 100% to +9, then drops
- Failure: No penalty (soft gate)
- Purpose: Gold sink for F2P/Dolphins

### 2. Refinement (Star 1-10)
- Cost: Refinement Stones
- Success: 50%
- Failure: Lose stones, gear unchanged
- Purpose: Material sink

### 3. Awakening (Tier 1-5)
- Cost: Duplicate Gear or Gems
- Effect: Visual aura + % stats unlock
- Purpose: Duplicate sink (gacha fuel)

### 4. Transcendence (Red Gear)
- Requirement: Full +15, Max Star, Max Awaken
- Process: Merge 5 Legendary → 1 Red
- **Risk**: Failure downgrades or destroys materials
- **Monetization**: Protection Runes prevent downgrade (Whale burn zone)

### 5. Gem Socketing
- 3 sockets per item
- Types: Ruby (ATK), Sapphire (DEF), Topaz (HP)
- Combine: 3x Level N → 1x Level N+1
- **Exponential Cost**: Level 10 = 10,000x Level 1 cost

## Material Sink Strategy

### Gold Sinks
- Enhancement attempts
- Repair costs after PK death
- Auction house taxes (5%)

### Duplicate Sinks  
- Awakening requirements
- Fusion material conversion
- Collection index completion

### RNG Sinks
- Reforge attempts for perfect stats
- Socket rerolls
- Upgrade protection consumption

**Key Principle**: Every resource type must have multiple competing sinks to prevent accumulation and maintain scarcity perception.
